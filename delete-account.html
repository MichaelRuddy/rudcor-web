Delete Your Account

BestBook by MudCor LLC

You can delete your BestBook account at any time. Account deletion is immediate and permanent — all associated data is removed from our systems instantly and cannot be recovered.

Option 1: Delete from the App

  1. Open the BestBook app on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings tab (gear icon).
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted.

Your account and all data will be deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at mike@bestbook.io. Please include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 48 hours.

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Account profile — email address, display name, and user ID
  • Usage data — all sportsbook link click history and interaction logs
  • Referral attribution — any referral code association
  • Authentication credentials — your Firebase authentication record

No data is retained after account deletion. There is no additional retention period.
