Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

BestBook ("we", "our", "us"), operated by MudCor LLC, publishes the BestBook mobile software application. This policy details our protocols regarding information collection, operational transparency, and data privacy.

1. Information We Collect

BestBook values user privacy and operates on a zero-friction data model. The application does not require account creation, user logins, or profiling metrics. Consequently, we do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, street address, personal phone number, or payment card details. Any local parlay building or tracking configurations you select inside the application remain securely localized to your specific physical device hardware. The Application utilizes localized, non-volatile device storage frameworks (including AsyncStorage and local device caching protocols) to manage user state configurations, remember interface preferences, and format onboarding navigation sequences. This tracking state is entirely isolated within your device's sandbox memory; no tracking identifiers, state flags, or behavioral profiles are uploaded to external cloud environments or synchronized across networks by MudCor LLC.

To optimize performance configurations across devices, we may aggregate completely anonymous technical usage telemetry data. This includes metrics such as application crash logs, hardware device models, and active operating system platform versions.

2. Location Data (On-Device Processing Only)

BestBook utilizes native device location services (via core device GPS frameworks) solely to provide real-time, localized compliance formatting, region-gating parameters, and state-mandated responsible gaming helplines. This location assessment is executed entirely in ephemeral, temporary memory on your local physical device hardware to determine your active jurisdiction. MudCor LLC does not harvest, transmit, share, log, or store geographic coordinates or physical location histories on any external servers or backend databases. While the Application triggers core hardware geolocation sensors to parse regional helpline configurations, this data processing window is completely ephemeral. No physical telemetry payloads or persistent coordinate histories leave your native hardware runtime context.

3. Third-Party Services & Affiliate Tracking

BestBook aggregates and displays publicly broadcasted sports wagering market data, including live odds, multi-leg parlay configurations, and best-value bet tracking vectors from licensed third-party sportsbook platforms. We do not process financial transactions, facilitate gaming operations, or accept wagers. All commercial interactions or conversions occur externally on the respective third-party licensed platforms.

We may partner with performance marketing networks to route traffic via outbound deep links. These networks may utilize basic non-identifiable tokens (such as unique click strings) to audit traffic conversion funnels. We do not share underlying user identity mechanics with these entities.

4. Data Distribution

We do not lease, trade, distribute, or sell user tracking configurations or technical data payloads to any third-party marketing brokers or outside entities.

5. Regulatory Age Requirements

Consistent with domestic compliance regulations governing sports betting markets, BestBook is strictly prohibited for use by individuals under the age of 21. We do not target, track, or knowingly retain information from minors.

6. Operational Changes

We reserve the right to revise this tracking framework to meet shifting mobile compliance standards. Any structural updates will be broadcasted transparently via the updated tracking version timestamp at the apex of this document.

7. Corporate Contact Information

For official documentation audits or security infrastructure inquiries, please direct corporate correspondence to our administration queue at mike@bestbook.io.
